Vian Nguyen

Graduated Maryland Institute College of Art / 2020

Vian is a queer Vietnamese-American game designer. They are interested in game design as a means to facilitate spaces of feeling, self-reflection, and noticing the world around us.

They are currently working at Harmonix Music Systems as a QA tester.
